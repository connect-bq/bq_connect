About Connect BQ

An intelligent urban mobility web platform to modernize and optimize the public transport experience in Barranquilla.

Our Purpose

Connect BQ was born to solve the uncertainty and long waiting times in Barranquilla's public transport system. We offer a digital tool to efficiently plan routes and a communication channel for users to report real-time events.

Key Functionalities

Multimodal Route Planner

Visualize public transport routes, stops, and estimated travel times on an interactive map to plan your trips with certainty.

Real-Time Citizen Alert System

Registered users can report and view incidents like traffic jams or accidents, fostering a community-powered information network.

Personalized Features

With your user account, you can access your search history and have the ability to generate and view alerts on the map.

Intuitive and Responsive Design

Enjoy an easy-to-use interface on any device, whether mobile or desktop, complete with a dark mode option for your visual comfort.

Developed in Barranquilla, for Barranquilla

We are a team of 5 Junior Developers who built this solution in 4 weeks, thinking about the mobility challenges of our city.
